About This Page

This page is about Skate 3 Saves The reason I've made this site is to gather saves and make them free to download for others to enjoy. Ever since EmarbyHD's website got shutdown I've been wanting to do this. So, now I am. This site is for PS3 Skate 3 saves like BLES00760 and BLUS30464. You can't get the saves off of your console to send unless you have Multiman, which is a homebrew app, but you can get the saves onto your console just by using a FAT32 usb.
